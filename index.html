Concept Projects

Snap Notes

Snap Notes is a productivity tool for students that take notes from online documents such as pdfs and slides. The main goal of snap notes is to alleviate the extra time spent when note taking, you can simply highlight any section of notes you feel is relevant for you to know and Snap Notes will automatically create a document with all of your highlights.
